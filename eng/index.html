DOO "MEBL COMERC"

DOO "MEBL COMERC" is a company that deals with processing of wood.
The center office is in Novi Sad, a plant in Backi Jarak. As part of the company is a primary processing facility (sawmill) and the final processing plant, where production is carried out various furniture and interior furnishing. Also, we have the vacuum dryer for drying lumber.
In the primary processing of most processed cent and oak, and if necessary, other types of wood. Products of primary processing (planks, half-, square-edged goods) are made mainly for the European Union market. Also, we produce various kinds of elements that are drought and continuing lengthwise (finger joints). From continuing lengthwise elements we make substructure elements for interior doors.
We also make a floor battens from continuing of linear elements as well as making various kinds of furniture battens. Namely, linear shaping element are coats veneer, mostly oak. Melamine veneer parquet slats get a new look and a new set of advantages of moldings that are made of oak. In the wood slats are no mistakes (nodes) there is no discoloration, and texture is similar to . Also there is no mirror. All battens are ready for varnishing.
In agreement with the customer is possible to supply and varnished battens.
